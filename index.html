About us

Kitenge derives from the eastern part of african language known as Swahili. Kitenge is an african fabric found in East Africa known as Chitenge, and the West Africa and Central African it is known as Ankara. We are a platform that supports and showcase the authenticity of the new upcoming modern african designers in to the modern world.
